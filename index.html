About the Project

The CERN NFT Project is a collaboration between the European Organization for Nuclear Research (CERN) and leading artists and technologists in the NFT space. Our goal is to create a unique collection of NFTs that capture the excitement and wonder of scientific discovery, while also exploring new frontiers in digital art and technology.
