About the Project

The CERN NFT Project is a collection of non-fungible tokens that represent the mysteries of the universe. Each NFT is unique and represents a different concept related to space and science.

The project was created by a team of scientists and artists at CERN who wanted to find a new way to share their research and discoveries with the public. By creating these NFTs, they hope to inspire curiosity and interest in science and the universe.
